The CTO sits at both ends of every task: deciding what gets built and
confirming it is ready before it goes live. Requests from any team reach the
department through **one channel** (never DMs) — see
[How requests reach us](/how-we-work/communication).

## How the team grows

Hiring is phase-driven — every role is justified by specific delivery work:

| Phase | Team size | New roles |
| ----- | --------- | --------- |
| 1 — The Websites | 2 → 3–4 | Current team; a second frontend developer once template volume demands it |
| 2 — The Client Portal | 4–6 | Senior backend engineer (first hire of the phase), part-time DevOps |
| 3 — The Employee Portal | 7–9 | Engineering manager, mid-level full-stack engineers, senior analytics engineer, QA + second designer when workload requires |
| Beyond | 10+ | Specialists as the work proves the need: dedicated DevOps, AI engineer, security engineer, data engineer |

If you are reading this as a new hire in Phase 2 or later: welcome — this page
should be updated with your name. That's a great first PR.
