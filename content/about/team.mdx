# Team Map

| Role | Who | Responsible for | Go to them for |
| ---- | --- | --------------- | -------------- |
| Lead & technical owner | Seif Eldin Sameh | Architecture, development, code review, deployment, priorities, reporting to management | Technical decisions, access/accounts, PR reviews, "is this ready to ship?" |
| UI/UX designer | — | UX/UI design, one consistent design language across all brands | Figma files, design tokens, brand visual questions |
| Operations manager | — | Business-side coordination | **Urgent requests** — anything that must jump the queue goes through here, weighed against what it would delay |

The lead sits at both ends of every task: deciding what gets built and
confirming it is ready before it goes live. Requests from any team reach the
department through **one channel** (never DMs) — see
[How requests reach us](/how-we-work/communication).

## How the team grows

Hiring is phase-driven — every role is justified by specific delivery work:

| Phase | Team size | New roles |
| ----- | --------- | --------- |
| 1 — Portfolios | 2 | (current team) |
| 2 — Expansion & platform | 4–5 | Senior backend engineer, second frontend developer, part-time DevOps |
| 3 — Ecosystem layer | 7–8 | Engineering manager, 2 mid-level full-stack, senior analytics engineer, QA + second designer when workload requires |
| 4 — Scale & geography | 10–12 | Senior DevOps, data/analytics engineer, possibly second EM |
| 5 — Consolidation | 13–16 | Specialists: AI engineer, security engineer, data engineer |

If you are reading this as a new hire in Phase 2 or later: welcome — this page
should be updated with your name. That's a great first PR.
