# How We Work

The processes that keep the department shipping predictably:

- **[Git Workflow](/how-we-work/git-workflow)** — branching, commits, merging.
- **[Code Review](/how-we-work/code-review)** — what reviewers check and why.
- **[Deployment](/how-we-work/deployment)** — how code reaches production.
- **[Meetings & Communication](/how-we-work/communication)** — when we talk,
  where we write.
- **[Definition of Done](/how-we-work/definition-of-done)** — when work is
  actually finished.

Process exists to serve shipping, not the other way around. If a process step
isn't pulling its weight, raise it — and update this section when it changes.
