# Adawy Group — Software Department Handbook

Welcome to the team. This handbook is the single source of truth for the
software department: **what we build, why we build it, and how we work.**

> **Move As One.** Businesses grow better when everything works together —
> that is the core belief behind Adawy Group, and it is also how this
> department operates: one platform, one board, one team.

## How to use this handbook

- **New here?** Start with [About Adawy Group](/about) to understand the
  business, then go straight to [Onboarding](/onboarding) for your day-1
  checklist. Goal: **first PR merged within your first week.**
- **What are we building?** [Products & Architecture](/architecture) covers
  the monorepo platform, the tech stack, and the reasoning behind every
  technical decision.
- **How do we ship?** [How We Work](/how-we-work) explains our Agile/Kanban
  workflow, the Trello board, code review, and deployment.
- **Writing code right now?** [Standards](/standards) and [Guides](/guides)
  are your daily references.
- **Stuck?** Check [Help & FAQ](/help) — including who to ask for what.

## The one-paragraph context

Adawy Group is an integrated industrial and business ecosystem — 38 companies
today, planned to grow past 100 — connecting manufacturing, packaging,
branding, marketing, technology, and AI under one infrastructure. The software
department exists to build the **digital infrastructure** of that ecosystem:
first the websites (one monorepo platform powering every brand site), then
the client portal that makes ecosystem membership a daily product rather than
a marketing claim, then the employee portal that brings the same clarity to
the group's own teams.

## Our documentation principles

1. **Why before what.** Every rule and decision documents its business reason.
2. **Docs as code.** Everything here lives in git; fix anything via a pull request.
3. **If it's not written here, it's not the process.** Found tribal knowledge?
   Add it.

> Spotted something wrong or outdated? Edit the page (link in the footer of
> every page) and open a PR.
